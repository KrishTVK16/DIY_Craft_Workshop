Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel or change your plan at any time from your dashboard settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your features until the end of your billing cycle.

Do the discounts apply to sale items?

Member discounts apply to regular priced items and cannot be combined with other major promotions or clearance items unless specified.

How do the free workshops work for Pro members?

Each month, Pro members receive a credit for one free workshop. You can use this for any in-person or live virtual session available on our schedule.

What happens if I forget to use my monthly kit credit?

Credits for the Pro Artist plan roll over for up to 3 months, so you never have to worry about missing out on a project you love.
