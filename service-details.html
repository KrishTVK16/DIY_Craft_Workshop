Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a kiln for this clay?

No! This is premium air-dry clay. It dries hard in 24-48 hours. However, it is not food safe for liquids unless you use a waterproof sealant (included in Premium Kit).

Is this suitable for kids?

Yes, absolutely! It's non-toxic and great for ages 6+. We recommend adult supervision for using the cutting tools.

Does it come with video instructions?

Yes, every kit includes a QR code that links to our exclusive video tutorial library.
